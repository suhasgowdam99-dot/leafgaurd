The Problem

Why plants die before help arrives

Traditional gardening gives you no early warning system — and when disease does strike, the treatments are imprecise.

Too late to act

By the time symptoms appear on a leaf, the infection has already spread. Precious bonsai and ornamentals can be lost entirely before help arrives.

Blanket chemical spraying

Spraying the whole plant wastes pesticide, damages healthy roots, and is especially harmful to delicate ornamental plants.

No environmental data

Without real-time humidity, temperature, and moisture readings, gardeners can't predict when conditions are right for disease to strike.

Technology

Built on proven components

Every piece of the system is purpose-chosen for reliability, power efficiency, and precision.

ESP32 Microcontroller

Dual-core, WiFi + Bluetooth enabled brain. Reads all sensors, makes spray decisions, and logs events.

DHT22 Sensor

High-precision temperature and humidity readings. Feeds the environmental context to the spray decision engine.

TensorFlow.js Model

Convolutional neural network running on-device. Classifies plant disease from a single leaf photo in under 2 seconds.

Capacitive Soil Sensor

Measures soil moisture without corrosion. Prevents over-watering and provides context for spray decisions.

12V DC Water Pump

PWM-controlled pump delivers precisely metered chemical solution. Triggered only when both AI and sensor signals agree.

Precision Mist Nozzle

Fine-mist nozzle targets only the infected zone — not the whole plant. Critical for delicate bonsai and ornamentals.
